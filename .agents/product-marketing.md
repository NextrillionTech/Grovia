## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** High-performing digital marketing is either prohibitively expensive (bloated agency retainers of ₹2L–5L/month with junior executors) or highly unreliable (cheap freelancers who lack holistic strategic alignment).
**Why alternatives fall short:**
*   *Legacy Agencies:* Charge premium retainers to pay for account managers and fancy physical offices. They frequently pass the actual client execution to entry-level juniors.
*   *Standard Freelancers:* Cheap but siloed. A coder cannot write persuasive copy; a writer cannot configure Facebook Pixels or Make.com automation workflows.
**What it costs them:** Lost revenue, high cost-per-acquisition (CAC), low website conversion, and slow delivery timelines.

---

## Competitive Landscape
**Direct:** Traditional Indian digital marketing agencies (e.g., SEO/Performance marketing firms charging ₹1L+/month retainers).
*   *Falls short because:* Bloated cost, slow execution times (weeks to launch a simple landing page), and heavy reliance on manual, overhead-laden processes.
**Secondary:** Standard freelance networks (Upwork, Fiverr).
*   *Falls short because:* No strategic accountability. The client must act as the project manager and bridge the gap between design, copy, code, and ads.
**Indirect:** DIY page builders and internal hires.
*   *Falls short because:* Startups lack the internal expertise to write conversion copy or optimize Core Web Vitals to rank organically.
