## Section 1 — Executive summary

### One-Sentence Frame
This 12-month marketing plan optimizes for transitioning Grovia Labs from a project-to-project, one-time web development agency into a predictable, recurring B2B growth engine generating stable retainer revenue through local SEO, structured cold outbound, and standardized conversion-optimization retainer models.

---

### Three Big Bets (Ranked by Leverage)

#### Bet 1 — Hyper-Local B2B Search Moat
Indian founders and private school administrators prefer working with agencies that have a local footprint. By verifying our Gurugram Google My Business (GMB) profile and optimizing our website architecture for local B2B keyword clusters (such as "landing page agency Gurugram" and "conversion rate optimization Delhi-NCR"), we can capture high-intent local inbound traffic at zero CAC, building a baseline of organic leads that bypass expensive ad auctions.

#### Bet 2 — Highly Personalized Outbound Engine
Traditional B2B agency outbound is spammy and fails. Our bet is that by scraping B2B SaaS directories, identifying Indian companies actively running paid Google/LinkedIn ads to unoptimized or slow (LCP >2.5s) landing pages, and reaching out with a highly personalized 3-point conversion teardown, we can achieve >30% call booking rates. Providing upfront, bespoke value instantly separates Grovia Labs from generic development shops.

#### Bet 3 — Standardized "Growth Engine" Retainers
Project-based builds represent high-margin cash flow, but suffer from low revenue predictability. We bet that by packaging a ₹20,000/mo "Growth Engine Retainer" (covering continuous speed optimization, monthly Looker Studio reporting, and bi-weekly conversion modifications) and offering it as the default post-launch step, we can convert at least 40% of one-time clients into long-term partners, securing recurring revenue stability.

## Section 2 — Strategic frame

### What Grovia Labs is, in one sentence
Grovia Labs is an on-demand B2B landing page design agency and fractional marketing team building custom websites, funnels, and brand systems for Indian startups, schools, and coaching brands at fractional freelancer rates.

### The category we're claiming
We claim the category of **Fractional Marketing Team & Landing Page Agency**. 

Rather than positioning ourselves as a standard dev agency or a generalist freelance developer, we merge the two: the business acumen, copywriting skill, and visual design standards of a senior B2B agency with the direct access, execution speed, and cost efficiency of a freelancer. We use custom AI-driven workflows to automate the boilerplate engineering tasks, letting our senior specialists spend 100% of their time on custom design, strategy, and conversions.

### Who we're for (ICP, distilled)
Our ideal customers are founder-led businesses and marketing directors in India (predominantly in Bangalore, Gurugram, and the Delhi-NCR area) who fit three vertical segments:
1. **Indian B2B SaaS & Startups:** Companies spending ₹50K–₹5L/mo on Google or LinkedIn ads who need high-converting, lightning-fast landing pages to decrease bounce rates and maximize demo bookings.
2. **Private Schools & Academies:** Education brands needing professional, trust-building websites and admissions funnels that capture parent contact info and drive campus tours.
3. **Coaching & Personal Brands:** High-ticket consulting, professional training, and cohort course businesses who need structured funnels to capture and qualify leads.

#### Stated vs. Actual Problems
* **Stated Problem:** *"Our website looks outdated, we need a developer to code a new one, or we need to rank on Google."*
* **Actual Problem:** They are burning ad budget or losing organic traffic because their site is slow, lacks structured copy, has weak trust signals, and redirects high-intent prospects to empty footers instead of active conversion forms or calendar schedulers. They want to buy a *website*, but they actually need an automated *leads engine*.

### The business model logic
Grovia Labs operates a high-margin, dual-tier pricing model designed to capture immediate project cash flow and establish recurring retainer relationships:
* **One-Time Builds:** ₹15,000 (5-page starter sites) to ₹50,000 (custom conversion-optimized funnels with custom schema, animations, and copywriting).
* **Monthly Retainer:** ₹20,000/mo+ covering custom content pillars, regular ad creative design, SEO, and ongoing conversion rate optimization (CRO).
* **Leverage Strategy:** We compress delivery times from months to 2–3 weeks using AI-driven workflows for code boilerplates and asset generation. This keeps our margins high (85%+) and allows us to offer agency-grade work at freelancer prices.

### Brand voice (the non-negotiables)
Our brand voice is **authoritative, professional, direct, and outcome-oriented.** We talk to busy business owners who have been burned by agency promises.

* **Vocabulary Rules:**
  * *YES:* Detailed teardowns, conversion wins, growth engine, custom engineering, scale predictably, LCP, outcomes.
  * *NO:* Synergistic, holistic, video audits, Loom video, vanity metrics, boilerplate.
* **Call-to-Action (CTA) Rule:** We lead with concrete value. Instead of forcing a sales call or pitch meeting, we offer a free detailed website audit/teardown with 3 actionable wins within 24 hours. The primary call booking link is a low-pressure scheduling link to book a direct 30-min Google Meet call.

## Section 5 — Activation

### Current state
Grovia Labs' landing page has excellent activation hooks: an inline audit request form, detailed services expander modals, and direct Google Calendar scheduling integrations. However, the post-submit workflow is manual. When a lead requests an audit, they receive no automated confirmation, and the delivery relies entirely on manual emails.

### The plan

#### Move 1 — Post-Submit Automated Welcome Sequence
We will wire a simple automated confirmation email triggered immediately after a visitor submits their website URL for a free audit. This email will set delivery expectations (24-hour turnaround), introduce Shubham Taneja, and share a link to our latest selected work case studies to build immediate credibility.

#### Move 2 — Structured 3-Point Teardown Template
To keep audit delivery fast (<20 minutes of founder effort per audit) and highly valuable, we will standardize the audit report into a clean, Notion-shareable markdown template:
* **Section 1:** Core Web Vitals speed analysis (LCP data).
* **Section 2:** Copywriting and B2B positioning feedback.
* **Section 3:** 3 concrete "conversion wins" they can implement this week.
* **Call to Action:** A direct button to book their Google Meet strategy call to walk through the implementation.

#### Move 3 — Indian Market Optimization (WhatsApp Chat Integration)
For the Indian market, particularly school administrators and local coaching founders, email friction is high. We will add a subtle, floating WhatsApp chat icon in the bottom-right corner of the website (`pointer-events-auto`, index `z-50`) allowing visitors to start a chat and request an audit or ask questions directly on WhatsApp.

#### Move 4 — Pre-Call Questionnaire Automation
When a user schedules a Google Meet strategy call via Google Calendar, we will configure 3 simple qualifying questions inside the calendar booking form:
1. What is your current website URL?
2. What is your primary business goal this quarter?
3. What is your estimated budget/timeline?
This ensures the founder is fully prepared and pre-qualifies leads before hopping on the call.

### 90-day activation moves
* **Weeks 1–2:** Build the automated welcome email trigger in Resend/Formspree.
* **Weeks 3–4:** Design and standardize the Notion teardown template for consistent delivery.
* **Weeks 5–8:** Add the floating WhatsApp contact button to the landing page.
* **Weeks 9–12:** Wire the pre-call intake questionnaire in the Google Calendar scheduler.

### 12-month activation outlook
* **Q1:** Automate welcome emails and standardize audit delivery. Increase conversion rate from visitor to audit request.
* **Q2:** Achieve an audit-to-call conversion rate of >30% by delivering outstanding teardown value.
* **Q3:** Implement a simple automated SMS/WhatsApp reminder sequence for scheduled calls.
* **Q4:** Audit and optimize the signup/contract signing flow for new clients to reduce proposal friction.

## Section 6 — Retention

### Current state
As an early-stage agency, Grovia Labs' historical model has been transactional (project-based landing page builds). We currently lack structured post-launch retainers or automated follow-up sequences to re-engage past clients. 

### The plan

#### Move 1 — "Growth Engine" Post-Launch Retainers
Instead of handing off the website and closing the project, we will package a recurring monthly retainer (₹20,000/mo) titled **The Growth Engine Retainer**. This will cover:
* Bi-weekly copywriting and layout modifications based on real traffic performance.
* 2 new ad landing page variations designed and built per month.
* Continuous Core Web Vitals speed monitoring and maintenance.
* Basic Google Analytics (GA4) traffic and conversion tracking maintenance.

#### Move 2 — Monthly Looker Studio Growth Reporting
To justify recurring retainer value, we will set up a standardized Google Looker Studio conversion dashboard for every retainer client. The dashboard will track three simple metrics: Total Sessions, Demo/Lead Form Signups, and Page Load Times (LCP). We will send a brief 5-minute video walkthrough of the metrics on the 1st of every month.

#### Move 3 — "The Growth Blueprint" Monthly B2B Newsletter
We will start a monthly email newsletter targeting our list of past clients, warm leads, and teardown recipients. The newsletter will share 1 actionable landing page breakdown, 1 speed optimization tip, and 1 local B2B lead generation tactic. This keeps Grovia top-of-mind for future project needs or referrals.

#### Move 4 — Client Milestone Check-Ins
For past one-time build clients, we will set up automated calendar reminders to reach out manually 3, 6, and 12 months post-launch. The message will simply offer a free 10-minute speed check and ask if they need updates, serving as a low-friction hook for upsells.

### 90-day retention moves

* **Weeks 3–4:** Design the standard Google Looker Studio dashboard template.

## Section 8 — Revenue

### Current state
Grovia Labs' revenue is currently driven by one-time web design and funnel build projects. While cash margins are high (85%+), revenue predictability is low due to the lack of contracted recurring monthly retainer structures.

### The plan

#### Move 1 — Tiered Pricing Standardization
We will standardize our project-based service tiers in all proposals and customer interactions:
* **Tier 1: Starter Landing Page (₹15,000):** A 5-page template-based site built on React/Vercel with basic SEO setup. Ideal for early-stage personal brands or micro-businesses.
* **Tier 2: Custom Growth Funnel (₹50,000):** Custom-coded, conversion-optimized landing page featuring Framer Motion micro-animations, full copywriting, LocalBusiness/FAQ schema, analytics setup, and a speed guarantee (LCP < 1.2s). Ideal for B2B SaaS, startups, and private schools.
* **Tier 3: The Growth Engine Retainer (₹20,000/mo):** Ongoing copywriting, speed optimization, and ad layout iterations.

#### Move 2 — Targeted Upsell Packages
For one-time project clients, we will offer two high-leverage upsell packages at contract signing:
* **Speed & Technical SEO Audit Package (₹10,000):** Comprehensive Core Web Vitals optimization, site speed fixes, custom robots.txt/sitemap implementation, and schema setup for existing sites.
* **Multi-Variant Ad Package (₹15,000):** 3 distinct landing page design and copy variations optimized for A/B testing on active ad campaigns.

#### Move 3 — Performance-Linked Retainer Bonus
For larger B2B SaaS clients, we will propose a performance-linked model: a baseline retainer of ₹15,000/mo + ₹500 per qualified lead generated beyond their historical monthly baseline. This aligns our incentives directly with client outcomes and opens significant upside.

#### Move 4 — Retainer Default Billing (Stripe Subscriptions)
We will mandate that all recurring retainer services are billed automatically via Stripe Subscriptions on the 1st of every month. This eliminates invoice collection friction, guarantees predictable cash flow, and automates payment recovery workflows.

## Section 11 — Marketing operations stack

### The thesis
By leveraging a modern, agentic marketing operations stack, a lean team (or even a single solo founder) can generate the marketing output of a traditional 5–10 person department. We achieve this by matching specialized marketing skills with automated APIs, MCP integrations, and AI-driven workflows to eliminate manual data entry, streamline outreach list building, and automate welcome and reporting lifecycles.

### A concrete example
* **The Outbound Pipeline:** Instead of manually searching for leads, our outbound workflow is built around simple automation. We use scraping tools to build a target list of Indian B2B SaaS startups running Google Ads. The CSV is uploaded directly to Instantly/Lemlist, triggering a personalized warm email sequence that references their real page load speed (LCP) and links to a custom teardown report hosted on Notion. When they click to book a call, the Google Calendar API triggers an intake questionnaire and posts the booking details directly to our Resend list to start the welcome email flow.

### Measurement — the metrics that matter

#### Proposed North Star Metric
For the current pre-scale stage ($0–$10K ARR), our proposed North Star metric is **Monthly Qualified Discovery Calls Booked**. 
At this early stage, maximizing high-intent conversations with founders, school heads, and coaching directors is the highest-leverage activity to validate positioning and close the first 3–5 recurring retainer clients. Once we cross 5 active retainer clients, we will transition the North Star to **Blended LTV-to-CAC**.

|---|---|
| **Acquisition** | Organic impressions (Google Search Console), GMB local search click-throughs, LinkedIn post engagements, Cold outreach email open and response rates. |

| **Retention** | Monthly Looker Studio report email open rate, quarterly check-in response rate, client contract duration (target: >5 months). |
| **Referral** | "Built by Grovia" footer monogram backlink clicks, agency-partner introduction calls booked, GMB review submissions. |
| **Revenue** | Proposals sent, contract close rate (target: >40%), monthly recurring revenue (MRR) from retainers. |
