## Section 7 — Referral

### Current state
Currently, referrals happen organically through word-of-mouth. We have no structured referral incentives, co-marketing partnerships with complementary agencies, or systematic placement of "Built by Grovia" branding on client websites.

### The plan

#### Move 1 — "Double-Sided" Client Referral Credits
We will launch a formal referral program for past and active clients:
* **The Incentive:** When a client refers a business that signs a contract with Grovia, both the referrer and the referred business receive a ₹5,000 credit.
* **Redemption:** The credit can be redeemed for one-time landing page updates, custom graphics, or applied as a discount on the next month's retainer.

#### Move 2 — Niche Co-Marketing Agency Partnerships
We will form referral partnerships with complementary, non-competing agencies in the Indian B2B ecosystem:
* **Partner Profiles:** Cold email outbound agencies, SEO content writing agencies, and paid ad management agencies.
* **The Loop:** They require high-converting landing pages to ensure their traffic/leads campaigns succeed (which we build). We require outbound, SEO, and paid ad execution for our clients (which they provide). We establish a mutual referral agreement.

#### Move 3 — "Built by Grovia" Footer Monogram
We will add a subtle, premium-looking signature to the footer of all client websites we design and build (e.g. `Built by Grovia` or `Conversion by Grovia` linking back to `grovialabs.in`). This drives highly qualified organic B2B leads who admire the speed and aesthetic of our clients' websites.

#### Move 4 — Post-Project Testimonial & Google Review Request
Immediately after handoff and launch checklist completion, we will send a structured feedback email. This email will link directly to our GMB review page and request a 2-sentence quote about their experience. Once received, the testimonial is converted into a card for our selected work portfolio.

### 90-day referral moves
* **Weeks 1–2:** Design the "Built by Grovia" footer monogram asset. Include the monogram clause in our project proposal template.
* **Weeks 3–4:** Draft the post-project feedback and Google Review request email.
* **Weeks 5–8:** Reach out to 5 local cold email/SEO agencies in Gurugram to pitch co-marketing referral partnerships.
* **Weeks 9–12:** Roll out the ₹5,000 double-sided referral program to our active client network.

### 12-month referral outlook
* **Q1:** Secure footer signatures on all new builds. Launch the review collection workflow.
* **Q2:** Establish our first 2 agency referral partnerships. Generate 2 qualified leads from footer backlinks.
* **Q3:** Drive 20% of new project inquiries from client and agency referrals.
* **Q4:** Scale co-marketing to 5 active partner agencies, establishing a predictable, zero-CAC inbound flow.

### Skills + tools
* **Primary Skills:** `referrals`, `co-marketing`, `emails`, `social`.
* **MCPs / APIs / Tools:** Proposal templates (Notion/DocuSign), Google My Business (Review links), GMB API (optional for review monitoring).
