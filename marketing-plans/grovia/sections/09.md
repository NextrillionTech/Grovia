## Section 9 — 90-day roadmap

Our 90-day roadmap is structured into four focused, bi-weekly and monthly sprints designed to establish our local SEO presence, automate activation emails, launch targeted outbound campaigns, and secure our first cohort of recurring retainer clients.

### Weeks 1–2 — Unblock
*Focus: Clear technical bottlenecks, verify profiles, and publish packages.*

| Move | Stage | Owner | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| Verify GMB & GSC | Acquisition | Shubham Taneja | Complete Google My Business address verification and submit `sitemap.xml` to Google Search Console. |
| Finalize Retainer Scope | Revenue | Shubham Taneja | Draft the detailed scope, deliverables sheet, and contract SLA for the ₹20,000/mo retainer. |
| proposal template update | Revenue | Shubham Taneja | Add the new tiered packages, retainer option, and upsell packages to our digital proposal template. |

| Move | Stage | Owner | Description |
