## Section 6 — Retention

### Current state
As an early-stage agency, Grovia Labs' historical model has been transactional (project-based landing page builds). We currently lack structured post-launch retainers or automated follow-up sequences to re-engage past clients. 

### The plan

#### Move 1 — "Growth Engine" Post-Launch Retainers
Instead of handing off the website and closing the project, we will package a recurring monthly retainer (₹20,000/mo) titled **The Growth Engine Retainer**. This will cover:
* Bi-weekly copywriting and layout modifications based on real traffic performance.
* 2 new ad landing page variations designed and built per month.
* Continuous Core Web Vitals speed monitoring and maintenance.
* Basic Google Analytics (GA4) traffic and conversion tracking maintenance.

#### Move 2 — Monthly Looker Studio Growth Reporting
To justify recurring retainer value, we will set up a standardized Google Looker Studio conversion dashboard for every retainer client. The dashboard will track three simple metrics: Total Sessions, Demo/Lead Form Signups, and Page Load Times (LCP). We will send a brief 5-minute video walkthrough of the metrics on the 1st of every month.

#### Move 3 — "The Growth Blueprint" Monthly B2B Newsletter
We will start a monthly email newsletter targeting our list of past clients, warm leads, and teardown recipients. The newsletter will share 1 actionable landing page breakdown, 1 speed optimization tip, and 1 local B2B lead generation tactic. This keeps Grovia top-of-mind for future project needs or referrals.

#### Move 4 — Client Milestone Check-Ins
For past one-time build clients, we will set up automated calendar reminders to reach out manually 3, 6, and 12 months post-launch. The message will simply offer a free 10-minute speed check and ask if they need updates, serving as a low-friction hook for upsells.

### 90-day retention moves
* **Weeks 1–2:** Draft the scope and service level agreement (SLA) for the ₹20,000/mo Growth Engine Retainer.
* **Weeks 3–4:** Design the standard Google Looker Studio dashboard template.
* **Weeks 5–8:** Set up a list in Resend for client and warm lead contacts.
* **Weeks 9–12:** Write and send the inaugural issue of "The Growth Blueprint" newsletter.

### 12-month retention outlook
* **Q1:** Secure our first 2 retainer contracts using the new post-launch package.
* **Q2:** Standardize the monthly reporting workflow. Maintain a client retention rate of >80% over 3 months.
* **Q3:** Expand the newsletter list to 200+ B2B startups and founders.
* **Q4:** Transition at least 40% of one-time build clients into active monthly optimization retainers.

### Skills + tools
* **Primary Skills:** `churn-prevention`, `emails`, `copywriting`.
* **MCPs / APIs / Tools:** Resend (newsletter distribution), Google Looker Studio (metrics reporting), GA4 (traffic and conversion monitoring).
