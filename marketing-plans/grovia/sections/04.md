### Current state
Currently, Grovia Labs' traffic is thin, driven primarily by organic direct hits and founder social posts on LinkedIn. No paid ads are running. While the homepage has strong conversion rates, it requires a steady flow of B2B startup founders, school administrators, and coaching business owners to build a reliable sales pipeline.

### The plan

#### Move 1 ‚Äî Local SEO & GMB Optimization
We will target local search queries in high-density startup hubs like Gurugram, Delhi-NCR, and Bangalore. By finalizing the Google My Business (GMB) profile and optimizing the homepage for local search terms like `landing page design agency Gurugram` and `B2B lead generation funnels India`, we will capture high-intent buyers looking for local partners.

#### Move 2 ‚Äî Founder-Led Authority Content (LinkedIn & Twitter/X)
Shubham Taneja will post twice a week on LinkedIn and Twitter/X, sharing visual "Before & After" landing page redesigns, core web vitals speed optimization walkthroughs, and actionable conversion design breakdowns. The posts will direct readers to request a free 3-point landing page teardown at `grovialabs.in`.

#### Move 3 ‚Äî Niche Vertical Landing Pages (Programmatic SEO)
We will create and deploy highly targeted vertical landing pages for the three primary customer segments:
* `grovialabs.in/saas` (targeting `landing page designer for B2B SaaS`)
* `grovialabs.in/schools` (targeting `school admission marketing agency India`)
* `grovialabs.in/coaching` (targeting `funnel builder for coaching brands`)
These pages will rank for niche search intent and serve as landing pages for outbound campaigns.

#### Move 4 ‚Äî Personalized Cold Email Outbound
We will scrape B2B SaaS directories (e.g. startup lists) and identify Indian companies running Google or LinkedIn Ads directing to slow or unoptimized landing pages. We will send a highly personalized cold email pointing out 3 specific conversion wins and offering a detailed teardown report, routing them to book a calendar call.

#### Move 5 ‚Äî Paid Ad Layer (Post-Retainer Unlock)
Once we secure 3 recurring retainer clients and unlock a ‚Çπ30,000/mo marketing budget, we will run targeted Google Search campaigns for high-intent queries like `hire landing page developer` and `B2B funnel design agency`, sending traffic to our niche vertical landers.
