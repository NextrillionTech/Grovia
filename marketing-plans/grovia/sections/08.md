## Section 8 — Revenue

### Current state
Grovia Labs' revenue is currently driven by one-time web design and funnel build projects. While cash margins are high (85%+), revenue predictability is low due to the lack of contracted recurring monthly retainer structures.

### The plan

#### Move 1 — Tiered Pricing Standardization
We will standardize our project-based service tiers in all proposals and customer interactions:
* **Tier 1: Starter Landing Page (₹15,000):** A 5-page template-based site built on React/Vercel with basic SEO setup. Ideal for early-stage personal brands or micro-businesses.
* **Tier 2: Custom Growth Funnel (₹50,000):** Custom-coded, conversion-optimized landing page featuring Framer Motion micro-animations, full copywriting, LocalBusiness/FAQ schema, analytics setup, and a speed guarantee (LCP < 1.2s). Ideal for B2B SaaS, startups, and private schools.
* **Tier 3: The Growth Engine Retainer (₹20,000/mo):** Ongoing copywriting, speed optimization, and ad layout iterations.

#### Move 2 — Targeted Upsell Packages
For one-time project clients, we will offer two high-leverage upsell packages at contract signing:
* **Speed & Technical SEO Audit Package (₹10,000):** Comprehensive Core Web Vitals optimization, site speed fixes, custom robots.txt/sitemap implementation, and schema setup for existing sites.
* **Multi-Variant Ad Package (₹15,000):** 3 distinct landing page design and copy variations optimized for A/B testing on active ad campaigns.

#### Move 3 — Performance-Linked Retainer Bonus
For larger B2B SaaS clients, we will propose a performance-linked model: a baseline retainer of ₹15,000/mo + ₹500 per qualified lead generated beyond their historical monthly baseline. This aligns our incentives directly with client outcomes and opens significant upside.

#### Move 4 — Retainer Default Billing (Stripe Subscriptions)
We will mandate that all recurring retainer services are billed automatically via Stripe Subscriptions on the 1st of every month. This eliminates invoice collection friction, guarantees predictable cash flow, and automates payment recovery workflows.

### Unit economics
*Note: Evaluated for a blended model of project fees and retainer contracts.*

| Metric | Value | Note |
|---|---|---|
| ARPC (avg monthly revenue per customer) | ₹25,000 | Blended average of ₹50K custom builds and ₹20K/mo retainers. |
| Blended CAC | ₹3,000 | Calculated from outbound tooling subscriptions, scraping, and founder time. |
| Annual retention rate | 80% | Target average lifespan of 6 months for Growth Retainer clients (equivalent to 20% churn). |
| LTV (rough) | ₹1,50,000 | Calculated as: `(ARPC × 6-month client lifetime)`. |
| LTV / CAC | 50.0x | Highly favorable ratio due to organic/outbound client acquisition and low overhead. |
