## Section 5 — Activation

### Current state
Grovia Labs' landing page has excellent activation hooks: an inline audit request form, detailed services expander modals, and direct Google Calendar scheduling integrations. However, the post-submit workflow is manual. When a lead requests an audit, they receive no automated confirmation, and the delivery relies entirely on manual emails.

### The plan

#### Move 1 — Post-Submit Automated Welcome Sequence
We will wire a simple automated confirmation email triggered immediately after a visitor submits their website URL for a free audit. This email will set delivery expectations (24-hour turnaround), introduce Shubham Taneja, and share a link to our latest selected work case studies to build immediate credibility.

#### Move 2 — Structured 3-Point Teardown Template
To keep audit delivery fast (<20 minutes of founder effort per audit) and highly valuable, we will standardize the audit report into a clean, Notion-shareable markdown template:
* **Section 1:** Core Web Vitals speed analysis (LCP data).
* **Section 2:** Copywriting and B2B positioning feedback.
* **Section 3:** 3 concrete "conversion wins" they can implement this week.
* **Call to Action:** A direct button to book their Google Meet strategy call to walk through the implementation.

#### Move 3 — Indian Market Optimization (WhatsApp Chat Integration)
For the Indian market, particularly school administrators and local coaching founders, email friction is high. We will add a subtle, floating WhatsApp chat icon in the bottom-right corner of the website (`pointer-events-auto`, index `z-50`) allowing visitors to start a chat and request an audit or ask questions directly on WhatsApp.

#### Move 4 — Pre-Call Questionnaire Automation
When a user schedules a Google Meet strategy call via Google Calendar, we will configure 3 simple qualifying questions inside the calendar booking form:
1. What is your current website URL?
2. What is your primary business goal this quarter?
3. What is your estimated budget/timeline?
This ensures the founder is fully prepared and pre-qualifies leads before hopping on the call.

### 90-day activation moves
* **Weeks 1–2:** Build the automated welcome email trigger in Resend/Formspree.
* **Weeks 3–4:** Design and standardize the Notion teardown template for consistent delivery.
* **Weeks 5–8:** Add the floating WhatsApp contact button to the landing page.
* **Weeks 9–12:** Wire the pre-call intake questionnaire in the Google Calendar scheduler.

### 12-month activation outlook
* **Q1:** Automate welcome emails and standardize audit delivery. Increase conversion rate from visitor to audit request.
* **Q2:** Achieve an audit-to-call conversion rate of >30% by delivering outstanding teardown value.
* **Q3:** Implement a simple automated SMS/WhatsApp reminder sequence for scheduled calls.
* **Q4:** Audit and optimize the signup/contract signing flow for new clients to reduce proposal friction.

### Skills + tools
* **Primary Skills:** `onboarding`, `signup`, `copywriting`, `ab-testing`.
* **MCPs / APIs / Tools:** Resend (for welcome emails), Google Calendar (scheduler rules), Notion (for audit delivery reports), WhatsApp Business API / widget.
