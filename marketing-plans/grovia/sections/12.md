## Section 12 — Tactical idea bank

### Overview
This section cross-references the 139 proven marketing tactics from the marketing-ideas library against Grovia Labs' funnel stages. It prescribes what is currently active (Now), what will be introduced as capacity grows (Q2 and Q3+), and what is intentionally skipped to preserve premium B2B brand voice and unit economics.

### Status Legend
* **Now (Q1):** Part of the immediate 90-day execution plan.
* **Q2:** Secondary layer to introduce once organic foundations are solid.
* **Q3+:** Advanced paid or programmatic scaling (post-retainer revenue unlock).
* **Skip:** Incompatible with our premium, high-converting agency positioning.
