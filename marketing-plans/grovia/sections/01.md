## Section 1 — Executive summary

### One-Sentence Frame
This 12-month marketing plan optimizes for transitioning Grovia Labs from a project-to-project, one-time web development agency into a predictable, recurring B2B growth engine generating stable retainer revenue through local SEO, structured cold outbound, and standardized conversion-optimization retainer models.

---

### Three Big Bets (Ranked by Leverage)

#### Bet 1 — Hyper-Local B2B Search Moat
Indian founders and private school administrators prefer working with agencies that have a local footprint. By verifying our Gurugram Google My Business (GMB) profile and optimizing our website architecture for local B2B keyword clusters (such as "landing page agency Gurugram" and "conversion rate optimization Delhi-NCR"), we can capture high-intent local inbound traffic at zero CAC, building a baseline of organic leads that bypass expensive ad auctions.

#### Bet 2 — Highly Personalized Outbound Engine
Traditional B2B agency outbound is spammy and fails. Our bet is that by scraping B2B SaaS directories, identifying Indian companies actively running paid Google/LinkedIn ads to unoptimized or slow (LCP >2.5s) landing pages, and reaching out with a highly personalized 3-point conversion teardown, we can achieve >30% call booking rates. Providing upfront, bespoke value instantly separates Grovia Labs from generic development shops.

#### Bet 3 — Standardized "Growth Engine" Retainers
Project-based builds represent high-margin cash flow, but suffer from low revenue predictability. We bet that by packaging a ₹20,000/mo "Growth Engine Retainer" (covering continuous speed optimization, monthly Looker Studio reporting, and bi-weekly conversion modifications) and offering it as the default post-launch step, we can convert at least 40% of one-time clients into long-term partners, securing recurring revenue stability.

---

### What Twelve Months Looks Like (Plausible Outcomes)
* **₹12,00,000 ARR achieved** via 5 active, recurring monthly retainer clients billing at an average of ₹20,000/mo.
* **Predictable Inbound Flow:** Grovia Labs ranks in the top 3 on GMB and organic search for core conversion and landing page queries in Delhi-NCR, bringing 5+ organic inbound leads/mo.
* **Strategic Partnerships:** A network of 3+ non-competing partner agencies (cold outreach, SEO writing, and PPC managers) referring client builds to Grovia on a structured rev-share or credit model.
* **Operational Automation:** Complete outbound pipeline automated via scraping APIs and cold-email sequencing, requiring less than 4 hours of weekly founder oversight to manage.

---

### 90-Day Priorities
1. **Unblock Technical SEO:** Complete Google My Business address verification for the Gurugram office and index the sitemap on Google Search Console.
2. **Automate the Welcome Flow:** Integrate Resend triggers to immediately confirm audit submissions, build trust, and set delivery expectations.
3. **Build the Outbound Infrastructure:** Qualify a list of 100 Indian B2B SaaS startup prospects running paid traffic and set up warm email automation in Instantly/Lemlist.
4. **Deploy Vertical Landing Pages:** Build and launch sub-pages customized for B2B SaaS, private schools, and coaching brands to handle targeted traffic.
5. **Launch the Review Loop:** Reach out to all past clients to secure GMB reviews and portfolio testimonials.
6. **Trigger the Outreach Sprint:** Run the first 100-contact cold email campaign, offering personalized teardowns to book discovery calls.
