### Measurement — the metrics that matter

#### Proposed North Star Metric
For the current pre-scale stage ($0–$10K ARR), our proposed North Star metric is **Monthly Qualified Discovery Calls Booked**. 
At this early stage, maximizing high-intent conversations with founders, school heads, and coaching directors is the highest-leverage activity to validate positioning and close the first 3–5 recurring retainer clients. Once we cross 5 active retainer clients, we will transition the North Star to **Blended LTV-to-CAC**.

#### Leading Indicators by AARRR Stage

| Stage | Leading Indicators |
|---|---|
| **Acquisition** | Organic impressions (Google Search Console), GMB local search click-throughs, LinkedIn post engagements, Cold outreach email open and response rates. |
| **Activation** | Free audit request form submissions, average audit delivery turnaround time (target: <24 hours), audit-to-call booking conversion rate (target: >30%). |
| **Retention** | Monthly Looker Studio report email open rate, quarterly check-in response rate, client contract duration (target: >5 months). |
| **Referral** | "Built by Grovia" footer monogram backlink clicks, agency-partner introduction calls booked, GMB review submissions. |
| **Revenue** | Proposals sent, contract close rate (target: >40%), monthly recurring revenue (MRR) from retainers. |

#### Review Cadence
* **Weekly Sync (Founder Solo-Review):** Shubham reviews the weekly audit submission volumes, cold outreach response rates, and local SEO impression gains on Google Search Console.
* **Monthly Review:** Review of client looker studio dashboards, Resend newsletter analytics, cash flow, and proposal close ratios.
* **Quarterly Plan Recalibration:** Re-evaluating growth priorities once active retainer counts change or when moving to the next funding/revenue tier (e.g. from pre-seed to seed close budget).
