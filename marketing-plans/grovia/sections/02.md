## Section 2 — Strategic frame

### What Grovia Labs is, in one sentence
Grovia Labs is an on-demand B2B landing page design agency and fractional marketing team building custom websites, funnels, and brand systems for Indian startups, schools, and coaching brands at fractional freelancer rates.

### The category we're claiming
We claim the category of **Fractional Marketing Team & Landing Page Agency**. 

Rather than positioning ourselves as a standard dev agency or a generalist freelance developer, we merge the two: the business acumen, copywriting skill, and visual design standards of a senior B2B agency with the direct access, execution speed, and cost efficiency of a freelancer. We use custom AI-driven workflows to automate the boilerplate engineering tasks, letting our senior specialists spend 100% of their time on custom design, strategy, and conversions.

### Who we're for (ICP, distilled)
Our ideal customers are founder-led businesses and marketing directors in India (predominantly in Bangalore, Gurugram, and the Delhi-NCR area) who fit three vertical segments:
1. **Indian B2B SaaS & Startups:** Companies spending ₹50K–₹5L/mo on Google or LinkedIn ads who need high-converting, lightning-fast landing pages to decrease bounce rates and maximize demo bookings.
2. **Private Schools & Academies:** Education brands needing professional, trust-building websites and admissions funnels that capture parent contact info and drive campus tours.
3. **Coaching & Personal Brands:** High-ticket consulting, professional training, and cohort course businesses who need structured funnels to capture and qualify leads.

#### Stated vs. Actual Problems
* **Stated Problem:** *"Our website looks outdated, we need a developer to code a new one, or we need to rank on Google."*
* **Actual Problem:** They are burning ad budget or losing organic traffic because their site is slow, lacks structured copy, has weak trust signals, and redirects high-intent prospects to empty footers instead of active conversion forms or calendar schedulers. They want to buy a *website*, but they actually need an automated *leads engine*.

### The business model logic
Grovia Labs operates a high-margin, dual-tier pricing model designed to capture immediate project cash flow and establish recurring retainer relationships:
* **One-Time Builds:** ₹15,000 (5-page starter sites) to ₹50,000 (custom conversion-optimized funnels with custom schema, animations, and copywriting).
* **Monthly Retainer:** ₹20,000/mo+ covering custom content pillars, regular ad creative design, SEO, and ongoing conversion rate optimization (CRO).
* **Leverage Strategy:** We compress delivery times from months to 2–3 weeks using AI-driven workflows for code boilerplates and asset generation. This keeps our margins high (85%+) and allows us to offer agency-grade work at freelancer prices.

### Brand voice (the non-negotiables)
Our brand voice is **authoritative, professional, direct, and outcome-oriented.** We talk to busy business owners who have been burned by agency promises.

* **Vocabulary Rules:**
  * *YES:* Detailed teardowns, conversion wins, growth engine, custom engineering, scale predictably, LCP, outcomes.
  * *NO:* Synergistic, holistic, video audits, Loom video, vanity metrics, boilerplate.
* **Call-to-Action (CTA) Rule:** We lead with concrete value. Instead of forcing a sales call or pitch meeting, we offer a free detailed website audit/teardown with 3 actionable wins within 24 hours. The primary call booking link is a low-pressure scheduling link to book a direct 30-min Zoom call.
