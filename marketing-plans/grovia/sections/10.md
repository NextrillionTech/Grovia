## Section 10 — 12-month outlook

### Framing
To build a highly predictable growth engine, we establish a **Goal-Based Budget Plan** mapped to achieving **₹12,00,000 ARR (₹12 Lakhs ARR)** in recurring contract revenue by the end of Month 12:

* **ARPC (Avg Monthly Retainer Fee):** ₹20,000/mo
* **Active Clients Needed:** 5 active recurring retainer accounts.
* **Monthly Churn Rate:** 20% (average contract life of 5 months).
* **Blended CAC:** ₹3,000 (Scraping tools, Lemlist subscription, hosting, Resend).
* **Annual Marketing Budget:** ₹36,000 (tooling and outbound software) + ₹60,000 (paid advertising testing starting in Q2) = **₹96,000 total annual spend** (+15% experimental buffer of ₹14,000 = ₹1,10,000).
* **Expected Growth Pattern:** Linear growth (adding 1 new retainer client every 45 days) punctuated by a step-function jump in Q3 once our co-marketing agency referral pipeline goes live. We will stage and layer three distinct S-curves: Outbound Outreach (Q1), Paid B2B Search (Q2), and Partner Co-Marketing (Q3).

---

* **Funding State:** Bootstrapped / Pre-seed (Cap Spend: <₹3,000/mo)
* **Focus:** Build organic search visibility, launch targeted outbound campaigns, and secure our first 2 retainer clients.
* **Outcomes by end of Q1:**
  * Gurugram GMB verified and ranking for local SEO search terms.
  * Homepage indexed on Google Search Console with sitemap.xml.
  * Automated welcome email sequence live in Resend.
  * List of 100 Indian B2B SaaS startup contacts qualified.
  * Initial outbound cold email sequence triggered.
* **KPI Targets:**
  * Organic Search Impressions: >2,000/mo
  * Free Audit Requests: >10/mo
  * Scheduled Google Meet Discovery Calls: >6/mo
  * Active Retainer Clients: 2
* **S-Curve Position:** Staging the **Outbound Outreach S-Curve**; launching the local SEO organic baseline.
