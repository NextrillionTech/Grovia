### A concrete example
* **The Outbound Pipeline:** Instead of manually searching for leads, our outbound workflow is built around simple automation. We use scraping tools to build a target list of Indian B2B SaaS startups running Google Ads. The CSV is uploaded directly to Instantly/Lemlist, triggering a personalized warm email sequence that references their real page load speed (LCP) and links to a custom teardown report hosted on Notion. When they click to book a call, the Google Calendar API triggers an intake questionnaire and posts the booking details directly to our Resend list to start the welcome email flow.
